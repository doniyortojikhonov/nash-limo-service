Policies
Billing
A 3.5% credit card processing fee applies to all Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and Amex transactions. This fee covers the processing costs associated with credit card payments.
Car Seats
SHM Limo Transportation will provide a car seat, if available, free of charge. The customer uses it at their own risk and is responsible for its installation and any associated liabilities.
Cleaning Fee
A biohazard cleaning fee of $500 per occurrence applies for cleaning and sanitation of any bodily fluids.
• A cleaning fee of $250 applies for detailed cleaning required if passengers trash the vehicle.
• A cleaning fee equal to 8 hours of the vehicle’s applicable rate applies for the ionization process needed to remove any smoke odor.
Drugs, Smoking, and Alcohol
No drugs, no alcohol, and no smoking are permitted in the vehicle.
Trip Updates (Additional Stops)
A $25 stop fee applies for the first stop less than 2 miles out of the scheduled route, for a maximum of 15 minutes. Wait time applies beyond the stop fee after the grace period ends.
• Any additional stops after the first stop or any stop more than 2 miles outside the scheduled route will convert the reservation to an hourly rate with the applicable minimum billable hours.
Lost and Found
SHM Limo Transportation is not responsible for any personal items left in the vehicle. If you believe you have left something behind, please contact us immediately.
Cancellation Policy
Full Refund: Cancel 12 hours or more before your scheduled service to receive a full refund.
• 50% Refund: Cancel between 6 to 12 hours before your scheduled service to receive a 50% refund.
• 25% Refund: Cancel between 3 to 6 hours before your scheduled service to receive a 25% refund.
• Cancellations made less than 3 hours before the scheduled service are non-refundable.
Frequently Asked Questions
What areas do you serve?
We serve the entire Nashville area, including Spring Hill and Columbia.
How far in advance should I book?
Please book at least 4 hours in advance to ensure availability.
How soon will my chauffeur arrive for pick-up?
Your chauffeur will arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled pickup time. We offer a complimentary 15-minute waiting period. Additional waiting time will be charged at the hourly rate for SUV and sedan services.

Yes, our hourly ride services require a minimum booking duration of 3 hours.
